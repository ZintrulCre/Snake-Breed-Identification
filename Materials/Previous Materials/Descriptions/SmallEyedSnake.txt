Habitats: Cryptophis nigrescens(small-eyed snake) is a species of venomous snake in the family Elapidae. The species is endemic to eastern Australia.
Scale: Small-eyed snake can reach 20 to 35 inches in length. Small-eyed snakes in the northern parts of their range are larger compared to small-eyed snakes in the southern areas. Males are larger than females.
Status: Venemous
