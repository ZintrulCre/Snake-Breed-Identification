Habitats: The checkered keelback (Xenochrophis piscator), also known commonly as the Asiatic water snake, is a common species of nonvenomous snake in the subfamily Natricinae of the family Colubridae. The species is endemic to Asia.
Scale: The checkered keelback is a medium-sized snake, but may grow to be large. Adults may attain a snout-to-vent length (SVL) of 1.75 m (5.7 ft).
Status: Non-Venemous
