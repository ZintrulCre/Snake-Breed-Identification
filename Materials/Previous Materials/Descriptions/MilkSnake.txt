Habitats: Lampropeltis triangulum, commonly known as a milk snake or milksnake, is a species of kingsnake. They are distributed from southeastern Canada through most of the continental United States to Central America, down to western Ecuador and northern Venezuela in northern South America.
Scale: Milk snakes grow to 20 to 60 inches (51 to 152 cm) in total length (including tail).
Status: Non-Venemous
